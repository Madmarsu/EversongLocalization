About Eversong

Eversong is a simple yet powerful app. Its main functionality is to remember a good song when you listen to it on the iPhone or iPad Music app. It automatically detects the song including the artwork from your music library. Then you can press the button "Remember this Song" and an entry in your history of songs is created.

Gestures

In the main screen you can use gestures to control the playback. If there's no song playing, a long tap anywhere on the screen creates a random playlists and starts playing. If you swipe left or right, the next respectively previous song is played. A short tap pauses the playback and if you swipe down the playback stops. And if you swipe up, you are switching to the Music app.

Link to Evernote

Furthermore you can connect Eversong to Evernote and create a note of a song there. You only have to authorize Eversong to create notes. To do so click on "Link to Evernote" in the settings. Various aspects of the note can be configured and there's an option to post notes automatically to Evernote.

Translation into your Language

Eversong is currently only translated into a few languages. If yours is still missing, we would be very pleased to include it with your help. More information can be found at EversongLocalization on Github.
